EE6301-Details

EE6301 is conducted for Electrical and Electronics Department Students (III Semester - II Year ) Under Anna University regulation 2013

--------------------------

The basic idea of the course is to expose the students with the basic architecture of the computer. The basic logic gates and FlipFlops addressed in the course forms the building block of the digital computer. in addition the methods of design of higher circuit of the computer would also be made familier to the students.

Assement Procedure

.

.

Assignment

Students are expected to design a small microcomputer on an FPGA Board using the concepts of Digital Logic Circuits. To encounter this assignment the students are adviced to work on MATLAB - System Generator interface to develope model based codes for the microcomputer architecture. the students after accomplishing the task are expected to write a game on the micro computer and play it ( Dinoser game).
