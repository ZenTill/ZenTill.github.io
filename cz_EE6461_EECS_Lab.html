EE6461-Details

EE6461 is conducted for Electronics and Communication Department Students (III Semester - II Year ) Under Anna University regulation 2013

--------------------------

Contails Experiments on Electrical DC Machines, AC Machines, Measurements, Instrumentation and Control Systems

EE6461 - Manual

EE6461 Manual was prepared with the idea of simualting all the experiments in matlab before conducting the experiments for better understanding.

--------------------------

All Experiments except few contains two parts the first part - Matlab Simualtion and the Second part - Experimentation. the users of this manual ( Teachers) are requested to conduct all the Simulation part before the Experimentation is carried out
